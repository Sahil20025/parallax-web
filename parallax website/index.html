Delhi India

Delhi Business

Delhi's IT business sector is thriving and rapidly emerging as a major national hub. While Bangalore holds the title of "Silicon Valley of India," Delhi offers a dynamic ecosystem for IT companies and startups. Backed by government support and strong infrastructure, the sector attracts leading domestic and international IT giants like Google, Microsoft, HCL, and IBM. The presence of skilled professionals, universities, and incubators further fuels its growth. With its excellent connectivity and ever-expanding territory, Delhi is poised to become a top destination for IT businesses in India.

Tokyo Japan

Tokyo business

Tokyo's IT business sector is a powerhouse, ranking as the top industry in the country. It boasts a massive and diverse landscape, encompassing software development, data processing, and information services. Major tech giants like Sony, Panasonic, and Toshiba call Tokyo home, alongside a thriving startup scene and a growing number of venture capital firms. The government actively supports this growth, aiming to push the industry value even higher. With a highly skilled workforce and a constant embrace of innovation, Tokyo's IT sector is poised to remain a global leader for years to come.

Dubai UAE

Dubai Business

Dubai's IT business sector is booming, fueled by a combination of factors. The government actively supports the industry through initiatives like free zones with special benefits for tech businesses. This has attracted major international tech companies like Microsoft, IBM, and Google to set up regional headquarters. Local startups are flourishing alongside these giants, creating a vibrant tech scene. The focus on innovation and digital transformation across various sectors further fuels the growth of Dubai's IT industry. With its strategic location and commitment to technology, Dubai is well-positioned to become a major global tech hub.
